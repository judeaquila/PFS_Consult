Application Status Guide
  • Pending: Your application has been submitted and is waiting to be reviewed by our team.
  • In Review: Our team is currently examining your application and verifying the provided information.
  • Approved: Your application has been successfully reviewed and approved. You're now officially registered.
  • Rejected: Your application was not approved. Specific reasons will be provided, and you may need to revise and resubmit.
  • Completed Documentation: All required documents have been successfully submitted and verified. No further action is needed from your side at this stage.
